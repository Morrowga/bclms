Can you explain what TiggieKids is and its target audience?

TiggieKids is a cutting-edge educational app created to assist caregivers and teachers in nurturing the growth and learning of children. It's particularly useful for children with special needs and young learners starting their educational journey.

The app features stories that use dialogical reading techniques and include AAC (Augmentative and Alternative Communication) symbols to support and enhance communication and learning.

Features & Contact

What types of educational content does TiggieKids offer?

TiggieKids provides a diverse collection of educational content, including interactive games, engaging videos, quizzes, that address a broad range of learning needs, designed to cater to different learning styles and stages.

Can you explain the interactivity features in the books?

The books within TiggieKids are designed to be highly interactive, allowing children to engage directly with the content. This interactivity includes touch-responsive animations, interactive quizzes, and activities that complement the stories. These features not only make learning fun but also support various cognitive skills, including problem-solving, memory, and decision-making.

How does TiggieKids support caregivers during reading sessions with children?

TiggieKids offers a unique feature that guides caregivers through each reading session with curated questions and helpful tips. This support is designed to enhance the interaction between the caregiver and child, promoting deeper understanding, engagement, and bonding. The guidance provided helps caregivers to effectively use dialogical reading techniques, encouraging children to think critically, express themselves, and connect more deeply with the story.

Is the content aligned with educational standards?

Yes, all content within TiggieKids is meticulously crafted by educational experts to align with national and international educational standards, ensuring that it complements formal education effectively.

How does dialogical reading work in TiggieKids

Dialogical reading in TiggieKids involves interactive stories where adults are guided to engage children with thought-provoking questions and prompts embedded within the story. This method encourages active participation, enhances comprehension, and develops critical thinking and language skills.

What are AAC symbols and how are they used in the app?

AAC symbols are visual tools used to represent concepts, words, or phrases, aiding communication for individuals who have difficulties with verbal speech.


In Tiggiekids, AAC symbols are integrated alongside the stories to support understanding and expression, making stories accessible and interactive for children with diverse communication needs.
